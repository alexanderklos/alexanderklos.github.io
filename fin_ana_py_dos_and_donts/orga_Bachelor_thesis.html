Organizational information on writing a Bachelor thesis

The supervisor allocation process is centralized and done by the examination office. If (and only if) you have been assigned Alexander Klos as your supervisor, the following rules apply:
  • The starting date will be determined through mutual consultation and agreement. It has to be within the pre-announced writing time period set by the examination office.
  • The Bachelor thesis can be written either in English or in German.
  • The topic will be determined by me after a short appointment. During this appointment, students will be asked about their general topic preferences (for example Finance or Decision Analysis).
  • I will not allocate topics that lie outside of my research interests or teaching focus.
  • Each student receives a short description of the topic (typically not more than three sentences) on the starting date and not earlier.
