Dos and Don'ts for Theses

The following HTML pages show a few dos and dont's for students writing a seminar, a Bachelor, or a Master thesis at Kiel University with Alexander Klos as their supervisor. To all other students: Use them at your own risk! Your supervisor may disagree.

  • Simple tests for theses II: Chi-square test of independence (Python, with an R oneliner)

Finance

  • Do not compound log-returns multiplicatively (Python)

General
