Organizational information on writing a Bachelor thesis

The supervisor allocation process is centralized and done by the examination office. If (and only if) you have been assigned Alexander Klos as your supervisor, the following rules apply:
  • We will determine the starting date through mutual consultation and agreement. It must be within the pre-announced writing time period the examination office sets.
  • You can write your Bachelor thesis in English or German.
  • I will determine the topic after a short appointment. During this appointment, students will be asked about their general topic preferences (for example, Finance or Decision Analysis).
  • I will only allocate topics within my research interests and/or teaching focus.
  • Each student receives a short description of the topic (typically not more than three sentences) on the starting date and not earlier.
